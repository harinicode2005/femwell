Daily Wellness Tips

Simple lifestyle habits to support PCOD / PCOS health

Stay Hydrated

Drink 2-3 liters of water daily to support hormonal balance.

Improve Your Sleep

Sleep 7-9 hours daily to reduce cortisol and inflammation.

Go for a Walk

Walk 20-30 minutes to reduce insulin resistance.

Warm Herbal Tea

Fenugreek or spearmint tea helps reduce PCOD symptoms.

Reduce Stress

Practice meditation or journaling for 10 minutes daily.

Protein in Every Meal

Helps balance blood sugar and reduces cravings.

Avoid Junk Foods

Reduce sugar, soda, bakery items and deep-fried foods.

Self-care Routine

Take time for yourself with skincare, reading, and relaxation.

Limit Screen Time

Give your eyes and mind time to rest.

Eat Greens

Add vegetables for a healthier diet.
